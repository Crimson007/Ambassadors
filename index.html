We are a group of enthusiastic and active individuals seeking to help members of our society in charity fundraising and volunteer activities.

We have numerous group bonding activities to liven up the group and also to take a break from the everyday hustle and bustle.

Our group consists of peoples from all walks of life, from students to working class citizens, irrespective of their cultural and tribal background. We also receive support from other organisations succh as Universities, NGOs, well-wishers among other partners.
